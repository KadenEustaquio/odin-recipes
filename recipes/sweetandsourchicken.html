Description

This recipe is to make sweet and sour chicken, a tasty asian dish with a kick! Don't forget to bring your appetite.

This dish makes for a great lunch or dinner, and serves plenty! It doesn't take up too much time or effort, but it's great nonetheless. Make sure to save room for extras!

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Pineapple
  • Orange Food Coloring
  • Cornstarch
  • Flour
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Egg
  • Chicken
  • Green Pepper

Directions

  2. Make a cornstarch slurry, then mix it into the sauce until thickened.
  3. Make the batter, then stir in the chicken pieces until well-coated.
  4. Fry the chicken.
  5. Layer the peppers, pineapple, and chicken on a platter.

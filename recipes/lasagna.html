Description

This recipe is to make lasagna, a tasty dish which is an Italian dish made up of baked meat and tomato sauce, alongside a cheese sauce.

This dish makes for a great lunch or dinner, and serves plenty! It takes a little bit of time, but it's worth it. Make sure to save room for extras!
